The Best Pancakes Recipes

The pancakes are the most delicious for the breakfast

The Ingredients

  • 2 Cup of Flour
  • Two Egg
  • 1 Cup milk
  • Pinch of salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/2 stick unsalted butter

Steps to Preparation

👉 Firt Step

  • In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt.
  • Add the egg and egg yolks, followed by the milk and 1 tablespoon of the vegetable oil.
  • When the oil starts to smoke, pour the excess into a small bowl for later use. Continue whisking until there are no lumps
  • In a separate small saucepan, melt the butter until it is golden brown, and whisk it into the pancake.
  • Mix Leave the batter to rest for about 15 minutes.

👉 Secund Step

  • Heat an 8-inch frying pan until hot.
  • add tablespoon vegetable oil.
  • When the oil starts to smoke, pour the excess into a small bowl for later use.
  • Return the pan to the heat. You now have a sheer coating of oil remaining on the pan.
  • Return the pan to the heat. You now have a sheer coating of oil remaining on the pan.

👉 Third Step

  • Pour 2 tablespoons of the batter into the pancake pan.
  • Tilting the pan to coat the bottom evenly.
  • Cook until the underside of the pancake is golden brown.
  • using a spatula turn it over and cook the other side.
  • Turn the pancake out onto an upturned dinner plate.

When finished, served the pancakes straightaway with the sugar for sprinkling and the lemon and orange wedges on the side for squeezing on the pancakes
